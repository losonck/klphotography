  <action>
Query the public DNS for `klphotography.ie` from a clean network and record everything. Use a public resolver to avoid stale local cache:

- `dig @1.1.1.1 klphotography.ie ANY +noall +answer`
- `dig @1.1.1.1 klphotography.ie NS +short`
- `dig @1.1.1.1 klphotography.ie A +short`
- `dig @1.1.1.1 klphotography.ie AAAA +short`
- `dig @1.1.1.1 klphotography.ie MX +short`
- `dig @1.1.1.1 klphotography.ie TXT +short`
- `dig @1.1.1.1 www.klphotography.ie A +short`
- `dig @1.1.1.1 www.klphotography.ie CNAME +short`
- For each MX target hostname, also dig its A record so we know where mail physically routes.
- If any TXT record looks like SPF (`v=spf1 …`), record it verbatim — Phase 5 needs to extend or coexist with it for Resend.
- Probe common DKIM selectors used by mail providers (best-effort, do not error if missing):
  - `dig @1.1.1.1 google._domainkey.klphotography.ie TXT +short` (Google Workspace selector)
  - `dig @1.1.1.1 selector1._domainkey.klphotography.ie TXT +short` (Microsoft 365)
  - `dig @1.1.1.1 default._domainkey.klphotography.ie TXT +short` (generic)
- `dig @1.1.1.1 _dmarc.klphotography.ie TXT +short` (DMARC policy if any).

If `dig` is unavailable on the operator's environment (Windows without WSL), fall back to:
- `nslookup -type=ANY klphotography.ie 1.1.1.1`
- `nslookup -type=MX klphotography.ie 1.1.1.1`
- `nslookup -type=TXT klphotography.ie 1.1.1.1`
- and the per-record-type equivalents.

Write the results into `.planning/dns/CURRENT-ZONE.md` with the structure below. Capture the **exact** strings, including TTLs and quoting. Do not paraphrase. Mark the snapshot with the captured timestamp (ISO 8601, UTC).

- <ns2.host.tld.>

## Apex (klphotography.ie)

| Type | Value | TTL |
|------|-------|-----|

| AAAA | ... | ... |

## www

| Type | Value | TTL |
|------|-------|-----|
| CNAME | klphotography.ie. | 300 |

## MX (email routing — must be preserved)

| Pref | Host | TTL | Backing A record |
|------|------|-----|------------------|
| 10 | smtp.google.com. | 300 | 142.x.x.x |

## TXT (SPF, verification, DMARC, etc. — must be preserved or merged)

(SRV / CAA / anything else returned by ANY — list verbatim)

## Notes

- DNSSEC: enabled / disabled (from `dig +dnssec klphotography.ie` — look for RRSIG / DS records)
