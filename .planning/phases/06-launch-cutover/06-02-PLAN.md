    Locked settings per D-04:

    - Apply HSTS policy to subdomains (includeSubDomains): YES — RESEARCH §2 confirms safe because bounces.klphotography.ie is MX-only (no HTTP server) so includeSubDomains cannot affect it
    - No-Sniff Header: YES (free security win)
    - Preload: OFF (D-04: deferred to v2; preload removal takes weeks-to-months via browser updates and is unreversible at human speed)
  </what-built>
  <how-to-verify>
    Owner steps:

    1. https://dash.cloudflare.com -> klphotography.ie zone (NOT the Pages project; this is the zone-level SSL/TLS panel).
    2. SSL/TLS -> Edge Certificates -> scroll to "HTTP Strict Transport Security (HSTS)".
    3. Click "Enable HSTS". Read and acknowledge the warning dialog.
    4. Configure exactly:
       - Enable HSTS: ON
       - Max Age Header (max-age): 6 months
       - Apply HSTS policy to subdomains (includeSubDomains): ON
       - Preload: OFF
       - No-Sniff Header: ON
    5. Save.

    Independent verification (run after a 1-2 min propagation):
    ```
    curl -sI https://klphotography.ie/ | grep -i strict-transport-security
    ```
